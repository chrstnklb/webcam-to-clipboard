What does this page do?
This app puts footage from your camera right into your clipboard!

No more annpying
"Save file to ..." -> "Find file" -> "Copy file"

1. Allow the application to use your camera
2. Copy the camera image into your clipboard
3. Switch to your target application and hit [CTRL] + [V]
Is it secure?
Everything happens on your machine!

No data is send to a server!
The camera picture stays on your machine and in your browser
Your clipboard content stays inside the RAM of your machine

If you are still unsure, you can check the code here:
https://wwww.github.com/chrstnklb/webcam-to-clipboard
